DISHI

 
Dishi is a Japanese restaurant, but in Japan, it was just a restaurant. We bring our products from Japan,
our chef is from Japan, and we make our soy sauce by ourselves - Just like in Japan. 
Only those who’ve been to Japan know the difference between Japanese restaurants around the world and local
restaurants in Japan. It’s a difference you can’t explain, but you can definitely taste it.
